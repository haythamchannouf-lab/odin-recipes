Caesar salad

This is an unbelievable, restaurant-quality, creamy Caesar salad dressing that will make you swear off of anything store-bought again. Great for your classic Caesar salad, or as a dip for all kinds of veggies. Will NOT disappoint!

To serve, toss with chopped romaine in a salad bowl and shave Parmesan cheese on top of salad; season with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Anchovy fillets.
  • 2 cloves Garlic.
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise.
  • 1/3 cup Grated Parmesan.
  • 1/2 Lemon, juiced.
  • 1 Large Egg Yolk.
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard.
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce.
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 head Romain lettuce chopped
  • 2 tablespoons croutons

Steps

To make the Caesar dressing:

  1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Whisk until smooth and creamy.
  4. Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly.
  5. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.

To serve:

  1. Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated.
  2. Top Caesar salad with croutons and enjoy!
